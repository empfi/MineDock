# MineDock Design

## Theme

Near-black canvas, white typography, cyan primary action, yellow secondary accent.

## Typography

Manrope throughout. Tight, readable headings. Short prose.

## Components

8–12px radii. Flat surfaces. Borders over decorative shadows.

## Motion

One coordinated load sequence. Fast hover transitions. No ambient loops.

## Layout

Asymmetric hero with real product preview. Generous rhythm. Minimal containers.
